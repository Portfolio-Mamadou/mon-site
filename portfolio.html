Bachelor of secondary education

This application is to display the list of candidates for the baccalaureate. A candidate can be added with the following information:

  • Full name
  • Notes of exam
  • Overall average
  • Jury decision
And possibly other other functionalities (CRUD) such as the modification and deletion of the candidate, with a search and pagination on the list. Depending on the candidate's grades, his general average is automatically displayed on the list, so the jury will decide whether he is Admitted, Sessional or Adjourned. And finally we will be able to obtain the candidate's transcript in PDF format, other functionalities will be added as the project progresses.
